Welcome to Arya's, Sankalp's, and Owen's Grades Website

Our Grades Website will help you improve your grades and understand how many points you need in order to reach your goal.

We have 3 modes available, each of which can help you in different ways.

1. GPA Calculator

- Enter in all of your grades in your classes to find your current GPA.

2. Points to reach goal

- Enter in your current points and total points (Ex: 85/115, 25/55, 250/335) and find out how many points you need to get to reach your goal.

3. Future Assignments

- Enter in future tests or assignments and see whether you can reach your goal or not.
